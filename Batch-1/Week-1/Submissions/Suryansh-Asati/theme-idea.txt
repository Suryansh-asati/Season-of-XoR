SignBridge
An AI-powered accessibility platform that translates sign language gestures into real-time text and speech, enabling seamless communication between Deaf/Hard-of-hearing users and Hearing users.

Guiding Principles:

User-Centric Design → Simple, intuitive, and inclusive interface focused on real-time communication.

Transparency & Trust → Clear functionality, reliable translations, and privacy-first handling of video/audio data.

Accessibility & Inclusivity → Designed to break communication barriers, responsive across devices, and compliant with accessibility standards.
